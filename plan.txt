Features: 
    JUMP:
        we will use space button or up arrow
        we should raise the space at the bottom, "bottom" pixels
    GRAVITY:
        we should lower the space between the dino and earth, lower "bottom" pixels
    Generate Cactus:
        Create a new html element with img tag
        Place the cactus at the right-side
    Cactus Run:
        We should lower the space between left-side and the Cactuses
    Points: 
        Every 1/10 of a second you get 1 point
        Keep track of the High Score
    Check:
        Check for collision
            When the space between the dino and the cactus is 0
            and the dino is not jumping
